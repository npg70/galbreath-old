***Christina Galbraith***, daughter of *Hugh Galbraith* and *Christina McDonald*, was baptized 15 Aug 1824 in Campbeltown.[^birth]
She died 10 Apr 1878 in Campbeltown.[^death].

Children of Christina Galbraith:

1. *Alexander Brodie*
