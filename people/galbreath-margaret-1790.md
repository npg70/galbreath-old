***Margaret Galbreath***, daughter of *[Peter Galbreath](galbreath-peter-1749.md)* and *Agnes McMurchy*, baptized at Campbeltown 9 Jun 1790, and died 25 May 1873.

She married *Neil Hawthorn*, a rope spinner.
