He married at Covington, Newport, Kentucky, USA 1 Feb 1936 to **Beatrice E Carroll** (Betty), born Cincinnati, Hamilton, Ohio, USA 18 Aug 1906, died Birmingham, Oakland, Michigan, 28  Sep 1976.
