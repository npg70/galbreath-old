***Jane "Jeanie" Galbraith***, daughter of *Donald Galbraith* and *[Jane Galbraith](galbraith-jane-1861-galbraith.md)*, 
was born in Patrick, Lanark 19 June 1890.[^birth] She was murdered in Glasgow 3 July 1914.[^death]
