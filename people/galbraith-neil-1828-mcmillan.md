***Neil Galbraith***, son of *John Galbraith* and *Mary Sinclair*, born at Kilbery ~1818.[^birth]  He died at Saddell 23 Mar 1874.[^death]

He married at Saddell 6 Feb 1856[^marriage] to **Isabella McMillan**.
