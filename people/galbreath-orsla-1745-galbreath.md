***Orsla Galbreath***, daughter of [Patrick Galbreath](galbreath-patrick-1712.md) and *Jean Watson*,
baptized 22 Sep 1745 in Campbeltown[^orsla-birth].

She married in Campbeltown[^orsla-marriage] to **Archibald Galbreath**.
