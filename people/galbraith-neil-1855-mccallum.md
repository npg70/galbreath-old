***Neil Galbraith***, son of *[Hugh Galbreath](galbreath-hugh-1823-mcnair.md)* and *Catharine McNair*, was born 12 May 1855.[^birth]

He married 24 June 1884 to **Mary McCallum**.[^marriage]
