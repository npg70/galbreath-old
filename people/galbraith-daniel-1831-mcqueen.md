***Daniel Galbraith***, son of *[Angus Galbraith](galbraith-angus-1784-mcleod.md)* and *Mary McLeod*, was baptized at Kilcalmonell 8 Jul 1831. 
He died at Campbeltown 20 Apr 1896.[^death]
