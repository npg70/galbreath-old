***Flora Galbraith***, daughter of *[David Stewart Galbraith](galbraith-david-stewart-1782.md)* and *Elizabeth Fraser*, born about 1821 in Campbeltown. 

She married at Campbeltown 20 Oct 1840 to Charles Campbell Hook.
