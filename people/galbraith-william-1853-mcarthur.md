***William Galbraith***, son of [John Galbraith](galbraith-john-1804.md) and *Janet Watson*, was baptized 7 Nov 1853 in Campbeltown.[^birth]

He married 7 Oct 1879 in Campbeltown[^marriage] to **Elizabeth Sillars McArthur**.
